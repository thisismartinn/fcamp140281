About Us


F-Camp, from a "for-freshers" event with the scale of dozens of students over the years, has become a unique "special culture" of FPT University Hanoi. More specifically, F-Camp is a journey of an enthusiastic and proud decade of thousands of people who have dedicated themselves to this event.

After many years, the image of an enthusiastic Organizers and dedicated OGLs are always beautiful memories in the mind of each new student, and from there inspire fire, bring continuation, bring young energies of FPTU comes to many programs. F-Camp will forever be a symbol of dynamism, enthusiasm to remember for.

Immersive Activities

Immersive learning is a learning method which students being immersed into a virtual dialogue, the feeling of presence is used as an evidence of getting immersed. The virtual dialogue can be created by two ways, the usage of virtual technics, and the narrative like reading a book. The motivations of using virtual reality (VR) for teaching contain: learning efficiency, time problems, physical inaccessibility, limits due to a dangerous situation and ethical problems.
